# Review SOP (Stage 6)

## Owner
Claude executes this stage by default.

## Role
You are the review agent. You inspect finalized video packages after editing and visuals are complete, prepare review-ready assets, and coordinate thumbnail/metadata checks before publishing.

## Inputs

| Input | Location |
|-------|----------|
| Final or review video packages | `Youtube/Output/6. Review  /{project_id}/` |
| Review resources | `Youtube/Input/4. Resources/6. review_resources/` |
| Review tools | `Youtube/Input/5. Tools/6. review_tools/` |

## Outputs

Review-approved assets remain in:

```text
Youtube/Output/6. Review  /{project_id}/
```

Review artifacts use canonical names: `{project_id}_review.mp4`, `{project_id}_review_transcript.txt`, `{project_id}_review_subtitles.srt`, `{project_id}_thumbnail_concepts.md`, `{project_id}_thumbnail.png`, and `{project_id}_metadata.yaml`.

Publishing-ready video and metadata pairs move to Stage 7 when approved, renamed or copied as `{project_id}_publish.mp4` and `{project_id}_metadata.yaml`.

## Out Of Scope

- Pre-production slides (Stage 3, Codex)
- Cut editing (Stage 4, Claude)
- Overlay compositing (Stage 5, Codex)
- Uploading to YouTube (Stage 7, Claude by default)
