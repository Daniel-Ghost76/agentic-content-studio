# Visuals Rules (Stage 5)

Codex owns and executes Stage 5.

## Stage Boundary

- Stage 5 happens after Stage 4 editing.
- Stage 5 starts from a cut video, transcript, EDL, or editing session output.
- Stage 5 creates post-production overlays, motion graphics, visual callouts, and visual polish.
- Stage 5 does not create pre-recording slides or presentation materials. Those belong to Stage 3.

## Output Rules

- Final overlaid videos go in `Youtube/Output/5. Visuals/{project_id}/{project_id}_overlaid.mp4`.
- Intermediate overlay renders stay under `Youtube/Output/5. Visuals/{project_id}/animations/slot_###/` unless a tool requires otherwise.
- Verify final video specs before handoff: 1920x1080, H.264, AAC 48kHz, 30fps unless Daniel specifies otherwise.

## Ownership

- Codex executes Stage 5 and Stage 5a.
- Claude hands off after Stage 4 cut editing is complete.
