## Search Instructions
1. Search YouTube for "[topic] [channel name]" or "[topic] site:youtube.com/@handle"
2. Work down the list until a relevant video is found
3. Aim for 2-3 videos total — they do not all need to be from priority channels
4. If a priority channel has the most relevant video, prioritise it even if a more popular video exists elsewhere
