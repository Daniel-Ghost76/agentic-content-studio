# 03a — Chapter Break

**Sub-library:** `03_full_screen_overlays`  
**Status:** draft

## What It Is

A full-frame section divider that signals a new chapter or major shift in the video. Dark background with a centred glass card containing a chapter number/label and title. Replaces footage for 2–4 seconds.

## When To Use

- Opening a new section of the video after a topic transition
- Introducing a new concept block, tool, or framework
- Breaking up a long video into digestible named sections

## When Not To Use

- Short transitions where a cut is enough
- When the chapter title is already spoken and visible on screen
- More than 3–4 times in a single video (loses impact)

## What Good Looks Like

- Full 1920×1080, `--bg` background or very dark footage blur
- Single centred glass card with chapter number (small, secondary) + chapter title (large, primary)
- Optional one-line description below the title
- Clean entrance: card scales up from 0.96 + fades in, background fades in behind it
- Hold 2–3 seconds, exit: fade everything out together
