# Visuals Sub-Agent (Stage 5)

## Owner
Codex executes this stage.

## Role
You are the post-production visuals agent. You work after Stage 4 editing has produced a cut video. Your job is to add visual overlays, motion graphics, composited UI callouts, and final visual polish.

This stage is not pre-recording slide creation. Slides, diagrams, and other materials Daniel records with belong to Stage 3 — Pre-production Materials.

## Stage 5 Activities

| Activity | File | When to use |
|----------|------|-------------|
| Visual direction and style | `Youtube/Input/2. Skills/5. visuals_skill.md` | Choosing post-production visual language and asset approach |
| Overlay compositing | `Youtube/Input/1. Sub-agents/5a. overlay_sub-agent.md` | Compositing motion graphics onto a cut video |

## Inputs You Work From

| Input | Location |
|-------|----------|
| Cut footage | `Youtube/Output/4. Editing/{project_id}/{project_id}_cut.mp4` |

## Output

```text
Youtube/Output/5. Visuals/{project_id}/
├── {project_id}_overlay_brief.md
├── animations/slot_001/{project_id}_overlay_slot_001.mp4
├── {project_id}_overlaid.mp4
└── {project_id}_overlay_check.md
```

## Definition Of Done

- Codex confirms the cut source path, duration, resolution, and fps.
- Overlay timing is based on the Stage 4 output timeline.
- Final overlaid video is saved to `Youtube/Output/5. Visuals/{project_id}/{project_id}_overlaid.mp4`.
- Output is verified with `ffprobe`.
- Sample frames are checked for visual alignment and readability.
