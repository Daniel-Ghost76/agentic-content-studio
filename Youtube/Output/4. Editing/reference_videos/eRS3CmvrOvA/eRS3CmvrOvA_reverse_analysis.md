## How to Apply to Your Videos

**Higgsfield clips (04d–04o) are the primary visual investment.** Budget 1–2 Higgsfield clips per skill section minimum. Use `04e_section_intro_circle` to open every section, `04j` or `04k` to explain how a tool works, and `04o` for the end CTA.

**Face cam overlays (02d, 02f) are high-frequency.** `02f_icon_pill` should appear whenever you name a concrete benefit (2–3× per section). `02d_install_command_bar` fires once per tool at the install moment.

**Grade:** Keep face cam clean and neutral — the Higgsfield clips carry all the visual richness. Don't over-grade the face cam or the contrast between the two will collapse.

**Zoom:** Nate Herk's ~16/min micro-zoom rhythm is a deliberate stylistic choice. It creates energy in the talking-head segments. Our current 1/60s rule produces a calmer, more measured feel. If Daniel wants to match Nate Herk's energy level, increase the zoom budget significantly — aim for 3–5 micro-zooms per minute as a starting point (keeping them small, ≤1.04x scale).

**Captions:** Not used in this channel. Optional for accessibility.
